  • SPM - SEKOLAH MENENGAH KEBANGSAAN PADANG SAUJANA (2022)

    SPM RESULT : 6A 1B 1C

    Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Padang Saujana, or its short name SMK Padang Saujana, is a secondary school located on Jalan Pejabat Kerajaan, Jerantut, Pahang. In 2009, Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Padang Saujana had 337 male students and 515 female students, making a total of 852 students. The school had 89 teachers. History : In 2002, the school secured 3rd place in the 3K Program at the national level.It consistently achieved excellent results in STPM at the Pahang. The school also frequently ranked among the top in PMR and SPM examinations at the Jerantut district level. In 2015, the school was honored as a Cluster School of Excellence.
